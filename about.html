About Vanaprasthashram

A place where senior citizens find care, comfort and companionship. For over three decades, Vanaprasthashram has been creating a home filled with care, respect and dignity for senior citizens. Established in 1993, our journey is built on compassion, family values and the belief that every elderly person deserves love, happiness and a meaningful life.

Our Story

Vanaprasthashram was established in 1993 with a heartfelt vision of creating a safe and loving home for senior citizens who needed care, companionship and a place to belong.

Built on a "No Profit, No Loss" philosophy, Vanaprasthashram is not just an old age home but a family where elderly individuals can spend their later years with dignity, respect and happiness.

Our aim is to provide a peaceful environment where residents feel valued, supported and surrounded by care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide elderly individuals with a caring, respectful and family-like environment where they feel valued, supported and loved.

Through healthcare support, nutritious meals, activities, celebrations and emotional companionship, we work towards improving the overall well-being of every resident.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a society where every senior citizen receives love, respect and care.

We believe every elderly person deserves a life filled with dignity, happiness and meaningful connections.

Our Commitment

At Vanaprasthashram, our commitment goes beyond providing basic necessities. We focus on creating a home filled with care, compassion and companionship.

From medical assistance and daily care to yoga, meditation, cultural programs and social activities, we ensure that our residents continue to live an active, meaningful and fulfilling life.

Future Goals

As we continue our journey, we aim to expand our facilities and introduce specialised care services for residents who require additional support.

Our goal is to ensure that every senior citizen receives continuous care, dignity and companionship while living in a safe and loving environment.
